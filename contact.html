Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom design take?

Custom pieces typically take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Rush orders may be available upon request.

What is your warranty coverage?

All TwinklyJewel pieces come with a lifetime warranty against defects in materials and craftsmanship. This covers repairs and replacements at no additional cost.

Do you offer resizing?

Yes! We provide free resizing for rings within the first year of purchase. After that, we offer professional resizing at a small fee.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Each piece comes with detailed care instructions. General tips: Remove jewelry before bathing or exercising, store in our provided pouch, and clean regularly with a soft cloth.

Are your gemstones certified?

Yes, all diamonds and significant gemstones come with official certifications from reputable gemological institutes. Certificates are included with your purchase.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary luxury gift wrapping with personalized messages for all orders. Perfect for special occasions.
