Our Story

Crafting Timeless Memories Since 2015

The TwinklyJewel Journey

TwinklyJewel began with a simple passion: to create jewelry that captures moments of pure elegance and joy. Founded in 2015 by master craftsman artisan Sarah Chen, our studio has grown into a beloved destination for those seeking truly exceptional handcrafted pieces.

Every ring, necklace, and bracelet that leaves our studio is a testament to our commitment to quality, creativity, and authentic craftsmanship. We believe that jewelry should tell a story—your story.

Our Philosophy

At TwinklyJewel, we practice slow fashion jewelry. Each piece is carefully designed and handcrafted to order, ensuring that your jewelry is not just beautiful, but also uniquely yours. We reject mass production in favor of meaningful creation.

We source only the finest materials—premium metals and genuine gemstones—because we believe your jewelry deserves nothing less than perfection.

Sustainability & Ethics

We are deeply committed to ethical practices. All our gemstones are conflict-free and responsibly sourced. Our precious metals come from certified suppliers who maintain the highest environmental and labor standards.

We also support local artisan communities and donate a portion of our proceeds to jewelry craftsmanship programs worldwide.

Meet the Maker

Sarah Chen - Founder & Master Craftsman

With 20+ years of experience in fine jewelry design, Sarah brings an unparalleled level of expertise to each creation. Her work has been featured in prestigious jewelry exhibitions and private collections worldwide.

Sarah's philosophy: "Jewelry is not just decoration; it's a wearable memory. Every piece I create is made with love and intention."
